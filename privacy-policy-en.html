Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

LiftPath ("we", "our", "the app") is operated by Krzysztof Heller, an individual developer. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the LiftPath mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

Account data: Email address and password (encrypted) when you register.

Training profile: Training experience, goals, body measurements, and exercise preferences you provide during onboarding.

Workout logs: Exercises performed, sets, reps, weights, and workout timestamps.

Device data: Device model, operating system version, app version, and crash/error logs (collected via Sentry — see Section 4).

Subscription data: Purchase status and subscription type, managed by RevenueCat (see Section 4). We do not store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Generate and manage your personalized training plans
  • Track your workout history and progress
  • Manage your subscription and app access
  • Diagnose crashes and improve app stability
  • Send workout reminders and plan updates (with your permission)

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. Authentication uses JSON Web Tokens (JWT). Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Workout data is cached locally on your device for offline access and synced with our servers when connectivity is restored.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Sentry (sentry.io) — crash reporting and error tracking. Collects device info and error stack traces. Email is stripped before transmission. Sentry Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) — subscription management. Processes purchase receipts from Apple/Google. Does not access your payment details directly. RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data — available via the Data Export feature in the app
  • Rectify inaccurate data — via your Profile settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data — via Settings → Delete Account
  • Withdraw consent for notifications at any time via device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at liftpath.contact@gmail.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. Crash logs in Sentry are retained for 90 days.

7. Children's Privacy

LiftPath is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
